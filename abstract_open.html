Abstract Submission

Abstracts for all presentations must be received by March 13rd (Fri) 2026. Abstracts must be submitted as a PDF document. Other formats will not be accepted.

Please prepare an abstract headed by the title, name(s), and complete address(es) of the author(s) and send it as PDF to sfp2026@ml.hiroshima-u.ac.jp.

Also, please select the session you wish to attend from the list below and specify them in your email.

  • S1: Long-pulse Operation and Detachment with 3D Boundary
  • S2: 3D Interaction Between Topology and Plasma Transport
  • S3: Magnetic Perturbations and ELM Control
  • S4: 3D MHD Equilibrium and Core MHD Instabilities
  • S5: Fast Ion Confinement with 3D boundary
  • S6: New Ideas of Divertor
  • S7: Integrated Diagnostic Development and Mapping Technology
