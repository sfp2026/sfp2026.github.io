Registration and conference fee

For registration, please click the button at the end of the page.

Everyone who plans to participate in on-site needs to register and pay the registration fee. Payment is by cash only. If you wish to participate remotely, please select "No" for Question 9, "On-site participation," on the form.

Registration fee in on-site participation:
Regular*10,000JPY
Accompanying person**5,000JPY

*Regular registration fee covers:

  • Participation in the conference sessions
  • Coffee breaks
  • Conference Dinner
  • Conference bag with Programme Materials

**Accompanying person’s registration fee covers:

  • Coffee breaks
  • Conference Dinner
