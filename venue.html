Venue

Phoenix International Center MIRAI CREA

The workshop will be held in Phoenix International Center MIRAI CREA, Hiroshima University, Japan. You can read more about this place at their webpage, https://www.hiroshima-u.ac.jp/en/miraicrea.

How to reach the SFP2026 venue?

The International Conference Center Hiroshima is easily accessible by public bus from the Saijyo station. The closest bus stop is "Daigakukaikan-mae (大学会館前)".

The information about the public transport and tickets:

https://www.hiroshima-u.ac.jp/en/access/higashihiroshima
